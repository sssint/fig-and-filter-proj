Transit & Parking

We are a 5-minute walk from the Weaver Street Station. Several bus lines stop just outside Chalk Lane.

Limited 2-hour street parking is available on Weaver Street. For longer visits, we recommend the public lot on the next block.

Finding Us

Look for the large oak doors and the smell of roasting beans. We are located right at the historic corner intersection, easily identifiable by our copper-topped tables visible through the glass.
