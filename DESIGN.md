# Fig & Filter Design System

This file reflects the customer-supplied brand and content pack. Do not invent additional brand elements, business details, services, or contact information.

## Brand

- Name: Fig & Filter
- Tagline: Roasted here. Baked here. Poured for you.
- Voice: Warm, plainspoken, and a little dry-humoured. Never gushing or corporate.
- Primary: Deep fig purple `#4A2C4D`
- Accent: Warm amber `#D9A441`, for accents and highlights only
- Background: Warm off-white `#FAF6F0`
- Text: Near-black `#221E1F`
- Headings: A serif with character, using a system serif stack
- Body: A clean system sans-serif stack

Only the four supplied colors may be used. Amber must not be used for body text on the off-white background.

## Global requirements

- Five pages: Home, Menu, Catering, About, and Visit
- The same five destinations appear in the primary navigation on every page
- Mobile-first, single-column layouts that progressively enhance at wider viewports
- Address and hours must be immediately easy to find
- No online ordering, table-booking workflow, or newsletter signup
- Use the customer-supplied copy without rewriting it
- Use the supplied optimized photographs from `content/images/` and their alt text verbatim
