Our Roots

The Frame & The Fig

Two years ago, this space wasn't filled with the scent of medium-roast beans and sourdough. It was an old, dust-quiet picture framing shop. We saw through the glass to a courtyard anchored by a single, stubborn fig tree.

We spent months peeling back layers of linoleum and scraping paint, deciding that if we were going to build a sanctuary for the neighborhood, it had to start with the same artisanal patience used to frame a masterpiece.

Today, that fig tree still watches over us, a living reminder of why we do things the long way.

The People Behind the Pour

We’re a small team of two, obsessed with the details that most people ignore.

Mara

HEAD BAKER & KITCHEN ALCHEMIST

"If the oven isn't clicking on by 5:40am, I'm already behind schedule."

Mara manages the heart of the shop. Every morning, she hand-rolls the croissants and prepares the seasonal preserves that make our menu unique. She believes in "slow fermentation and fast service."

Dev

MASTER ROASTER & LOGISTICS

"Twice a week, the neighborhood smells like home."

Dev is the guardian of the bean. He roasts in small batches on Tuesday and Friday afternoons. His dry humor is almost as sharp as his palate for Ethiopian micro-lots.
